PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Last Updated: June 23, 2026

Alchemical Solutions LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you download, install, play, or interact with our mobile game, 9 to Zen™, our websites, and our cloud-based backend infrastructure (collectively, the "Services").

By accessing or using the Services, you agree to the collection and use of your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use the Services.

1. CHILDREN & MINOR USERS (COPPA / GDPR COMPLIANCE)

1.1 COPPA and Age-Gating

9 to Zen™ is a general audience puzzle game. We do not knowingly collect, solicit, or track "Personal Information" (as defined under the US Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act, "COPPA") from children under the age of 13, or the equivalent minimum legal age in your international jurisdiction (e.g., 16 in certain EU member states under GDPR), without verifiable parental consent.

1.2 Data Limitations for Minors

If our systems detect, or if we are notified, that a user is under the required age of legal majority:

  • We will restrict the collection of persistent identifiers to those necessary solely to support internal game operations (e.g., local gameplay analytics or local save states).
  • We will disable behavioral or targeted third-party advertising tracking for that device.
  • Parents or legal guardians can review, update, or request the deletion of any data tied to their child's profile by contacting us at the address provided in Section 10.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT & HOW WE COLLECT IT

Our data collection strategy is engineered around a "Guest First" frictionless framework to minimize the collection of personally identifying data.

2.1 Information Collected Dynamically (Anonymous Seekers)

If you play strictly as an "Anonymous Seeker", no registration, name, email, or password is collected. All game progress, your "Trove tile count", and High Scores are saved locally on your device. However, to facilitate baseline gameplay and serve advertisements, we automatically capture device-level data via third-party SDKs, including:

  • IP address and rough geographic location (country/city level).
  • Mobile Device Identifiers (e.g., Apple IDFA, Google Advertising ID).
  • Device hardware details (model, operating system version, screen resolution).
  • In-game event data (levels cleared, tile fractions earned, daily streak matrix loops).

2.2 Information Collected via Cloud Sync (Zen Profile)

If you opt to protect your game data using our "Cloud Sync" feature, you establish a "Zen Profile" managed by our backend partner, Microsoft PlayFab. When you authenticate, we collect and store:

  • A randomized unique cloud user ID linked securely to your device.
  • Active streak metrics, milestone multipliers, and leaderboard submission names.
  • Entitlement markers validating your "Ascend Level" (Pro Level) or trial version purchases.

2.3 Financial Data (In-App Purchases)

All financial transactions for Ascend Level access ($9.99 USD) or trial conversions are processed directly and exclusively by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Alchemical Solutions LLC does not collect, store, or process your credit card numbers, billing addresses, or bank account information. We receive only a digital token verifying payment validation from the respective app platform.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We process and utilize data strictly to fulfill your user experience and secure company operations, including to:

  • Initialize, synchronize, and update active daily streak data via Microsoft PlayFab upon secure app initialization.
  • Maintain global, cross-platform leaderboards (displaying your chosen public Zen Profile name and high scores).
  • Power our ad-supported gameplay tier using intermittent and optional rewarded video ads.
  • Protect the integrity of the game's economy, monitoring for unauthorized manipulation of "magic 9 tiles" or Streak Protection Shields.

4. SHARING AND DISCLOSING DATA

We do not sell your personal data for cash. However, to serve ads to users who have not unlocked the Ascend Level tier, we share device tracking identifiers with third-party digital advertising networks.

4.1 Third-Party Ad Networks & SDKs

Our ad ecosystem incorporates partners including, but not limited to: Google AdMob, AppLovin, Unity Ads, Liftoff Monetize (Vungle), Mintegral, Pangle, and InMobi. These networks utilize unique advertising tracking IDs to personalize and serve contextual or behavioral ads.

4.2 Cloud Infrastructure Backend

Your player profiles, scores, and streak data are transferred to and processed by Microsoft PlayFab to enable continuous multi-device sync and automated backend functions.

4.3 Automated & Algorithmic Moderation

We disclose user-generated text inputs (such as custom leaderboard names) to automated screening algorithms and tools to automatically purge profanity, hate speech, or trademark violations before names go public.

5. GLOBAL JURISDICTIONAL PRIVACY RIGHTS

5.1 United States Privacy Rights (CPRA, VCDPA, etc.)

Residents of California, Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, and other applicable states possess specific data controls. Under modern US state privacy laws, our distribution of tracking IDs to ad networks may constitute a "sale" or "sharing" of data for cross-context behavioral advertising.

  • Your Rights: You have the right to know what data we collect, request deletion, and opt out of the sharing of data for targeted ads.
  • How to Exercise: You may opt out at any time via the in-game settings panel of 9 to Zen™ or by adjusting the "Limit Ad Tracking" or "Allow Apps to Request to Track" settings directly in your iOS or Android system privacy configurations.

5.2 International Users (EEA, UK, and Switzerland)

If you access 9 to Zen™ from the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, your data is processed outside your home territory and moved to servers in the United States.

  • Legal Basis: We process data to execute our contract with you (the Terms of Service), or based on your consent where required by your device configuration.
  • Data Transfer Guards: Data sent to Microsoft PlayFab or our ad partners relies on validated legal mechanisms, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs). You hold the right to access, rectify, restrict, or erase your data profile by requesting an account wipe through customer support.

6. REBALANCE & LIFECYCLE EXTINCTION PROVISIONS

6.1 Virtual Item Zero-Value Policy

All personal data, metrics, and profiles collected by our servers are tied exclusively to digital virtual features ("magic 9 tiles", Streak Protection Shields). As defined in our Terms of Service, these metrics hold no real-world value or financial equity. Consequently, any adjustment, wipe, reset, or compression of game statistics or balances does not constitute a loss or destruction of personal property or compensable data assets.

6.2 Life-of-the-Game Sunset Clause

CRITICAL DISCLOSURE: All data storage, profile recovery capabilities, and backend cloud syncing functions are strictly guaranteed only for the active operational lifespan of 9 to Zen™. If Alchemical Solutions LLC decides, at its sole discretion, to permanently take down, discontinue, or sunset 9 to Zen™, all user profiles, metadata, high scores, and tracking files stored via Microsoft PlayFab or our servers may be permanently and irrevocably deleted without liability, compensation, or notification to you.

7. SECURITY AND RETENTION

We employ industry-standard encryption protocols during data transmission and server storage. We retain Zen Profile and gameplay metadata for as long as your account remains actively engaged with our servers. If a profile remains dormant with zero cloud login initializations for a rolling period exceeding twelve (12) consecutive months, we reserve the right to prune and permanently wipe that file from our active storage arrays to ensure data minimization.

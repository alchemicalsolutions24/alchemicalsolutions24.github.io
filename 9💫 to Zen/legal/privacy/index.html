Part 1: Global Privacy Policy

This section applies to all players who download, install, or play 9 to Zen™, regardless of geographic location.

1. Data We Collect and How We Collect It

Because 9 to Zen™ uses a "Guest First" architecture, we do not require you to provide your proper legal name, real-world address, or email to play. However, our systems automatically collect certain digital identifiers to operate the game safely and display ads.

A. Information Collected Automatically

When you interact with the Services, our game-focused backend service provider (Microsoft PlayFab) and our third-party advertising partners automatically log:

  • Device Identifiers: Your IP address, Internet Service Provider (ISP), hardware model, operating system version, and unique device tracking tokens (such as Apple's IDFA or Google's Advertising ID / GAID).
  • Gameplay Telemetry & Metrics: Your custom public leaderboard name (or your fallback placeholder like "Anonymous Seeker"), high scores, active puzzle matrix progression, total "Trove tile counts," and active daily login streak loops.
  • Network & Connection Logs: Time and date stamps of your game initialization, server requests, and network connection status.

B. Information You Provide Voluntarily

If you contact our customer support team directly via email, we will collect your email address, your platform store billing transaction history (for resolving purchase disputes), and any text or screenshots you send to help us troubleshoot your technical issues.

2. How We Use Your Data

We process the data we collect for the following core, gameplay-focused operational purposes:

  • Executing the Game Flow: Synchronizing your Zen Profile, milestones, and puzzle data across multiple devices via our Microsoft PlayFab backend.
  • Maintaining Fair Play: Analyzing score velocity and telemetry to prevent cheating, hacking, or time-traveling clock exploits on our global leaderboards.
  • Monetization and Ad Operations: Serving intermittent interstitial, banner, and optional rewarded ads to keep the basic version of the game free to play.
  • Customer Support: Resolving platform transaction discrepancies or processing voluntary profile modifications.

3. Sharing and Disclosing Data

We do not sell your personal data to data brokers for cash. However, we do share specific digital identifiers with trusted third-party service providers to manage the game application:

  • Cloud Backend Infrastructure: We transmit and store your gameplay data securely using Microsoft PlayFab servers.
  • Advertising Partners: We integrate SDKs from major mobile ad networks to deliver in-game marketing. These partners include: Google AdMob, AppLovin, Unity Ads, Liftoff Monetize (Vungle), Mintegral, Pangle, and InMobi. These networks collect device tracking tokens to determine ad placement and performance metrics.

5. Notice of "Selling" or "Sharing" Data

Under the CCPA, "selling" data includes disclosing it to a third party for monetary or valuable consideration, and "sharing" refers explicitly to disclosing data for cross-context behavioral advertising.

  • We do not "sell" your personal information for cash.
  • We do "share" your device identifiers (IDFA/GAID) and coarse location data with our third-party advertising partners to deliver personalized, behavioral advertisements. This activity qualifies as a "sale" or "share" under CCPA/CPRA definitions.

6. Your Rights Under California Law

If you are a resident of California, you possess the following statutory rights regarding your data:

  • The Right to Know: You may request that we disclose what specific personal information we have collected, used, and shared about you over the past 12 months.
  • The Right to Delete: You may request that we delete the personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain legal exceptions (such as maintaining active transactions or preventing fraud).
  • The Right to Opt-Out of Selling or Sharing: You have the absolute right to direct us to stop sharing your data for personalized, targeted advertising.
  • The Right to Non-Discrimination: We will never penalize you, alter your game mechanics, or restrict your ability to play 9 to Zen™ if you choose to exercise your CCPA privacy rights.

7. How to Exercise Your Rights (CCPA)

  • To Opt-Out of Personalization: Open the game application, navigate to the Settings menu, select Legal & Privacy, and adjust your preferences within the Privacy Center toggle interface. Alternatively, you can utilize the native "Limit Ad Tracking" settings located within your iOS or Android system device configurations.
  • To Request Deletion or Access: Send a formal written request via email to our privacy team at alchemicalsolutions24@gmail.com. Because your game account is managed anonymously via Microsoft PlayFab, you must provide your exact, unique Zen Profile ID or a screenshot of your localized settings string to enable us to locate and verify your data record.

9. Lawful Bases for Data Processing

Under GDPR Article 6, we must rely on explicit legal frameworks to handle your data. We process your data under the following conditions:

  • Performance of a Contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): We process your core gameplay data, save states, and leaderboard rankings via Microsoft PlayFab because it is strictly necessary to deliver the gameplay services, protect your active streak configurations, and execute the consumer contract you initiated by downloading the software.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): We and our advertising networks process your unique mobile advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID) for personalized, behavioral advertising only if you provide explicit, affirmative consent via our in-game GDPR compliance tracking pop-up overlay during your initial application startup.
  • Legitimate Interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): We process basic network telemetry and app stability logs to safeguard our platform security, balance our internal virtual tile economy, and identify fraudulent leaderboard manipulation exploits.

10. Your Rights Under the GDPR

As a data subject in the EU or UK, you possess comprehensive rights protecting your digital identity:

  • Right of Access (Art. 15): The right to request copies of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Erasure / "Right to be Forgotten" (Art. 17): The right to request that we wipe your Zen Profile cloud save data from our backend servers.
  • Right to Restrict Processing (Art. 18): The right to demand that we stop modifying or utilizing your data while a legal dispute is pending.
  • Right to Data Portability (Art. 20): The right to receive your structured gameplay data in a clean, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent (Art. 7(3)): The right to revoke your marketing tracking choices at any exact moment.

11. International Data Transfers

Alchemical Solutions LLC operates out of the United States. Gameplay metrics and profile sync fields captured within 9 to Zen™ are transferred to and processed on secure servers located within the United States via our infrastructure host, Microsoft PlayFab.

To ensure your data remains protected when crossing international borders, we rely on standard contract safeguards, including the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs), incorporated directly into our agreements with third-party service providers.

12. Exercising Your GDPR Rights and Data Retention

  • Withdrawing Consent: You can instantly stop targeted ad tracking at any time by going to Settings > Legal & Privacy > Privacy Center and toggling off personalized advertising tracking.
  • Data Requests & Erasure: To completely wipe your data or request an access report, submit an email request to [Insert Privacy Email]. To safeguard user identity, you must provide your anonymous internal Zen Profile ID code so we can verify the link between your device and the cloud record.
  • Data Lifespan Threshold: If your Zen Profile remains completely dormant on our Microsoft PlayFab servers for a rolling period exceeding twelve (12) consecutive months, your records will be flagged for automatic data cleansing and permanently erased, instantly terminating storage requirements under this policy.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: If you believe our data processing operations violate GDPR mandates, you possess a legal right to lodge a formal complaint with a supervisory Data Protection Authority (DPA) located within your specific EU Member State or UK residence.

Part I: Community & Leaderboard Rules

1. User-Generated Text & Profile Names

When customizing your public Zen Profile name for leaderboard display, you are strictly prohibited from utilizing any text, symbols, or combinations of characters that contain, promote, or depict:

  • Profanity, vulgarity, or sexually explicit content.
  • Hate speech, discriminatory language, or slurs targeting race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, or disability.
  • Harassment, bullying, stalking, or targeted defamation of other players or third parties.
  • Unauthorized trademarks, copyrighted properties, or celebrity brand assets that you do not own.
  • Impersonation of Alchemical Solutions LLC staff, developers, or platform administrators.

2. Automated Tracking & Algorithmic Moderation

To enforce these standards at scale, Alchemical Solutions LLC utilizes automated text screening algorithms and automated moderation tools during the profile naming process.

  • Unilateral Enforcement: We reserve the absolute, unilateral right to filter, reject, or modify any user-submitted profile name proactively.
  • Randomized Placeholders: If our systems flag your chosen name as an infraction of our community standards, your public leaderboard identifier will be instantly and automatically reverted to "Anonymous Seeker" or a randomized generic placeholder, without prior notice or right of appeal.

3. Competitive Integrity & Fair Play

The integrity of the 9 to Zen™ puzzle matrix, daily streak counts, and leaderboards is paramount. Any attempt to artificially manipulate game data or cloud scores is strictly forbidden. Prohibited activities include:

  • Utilizing third-party software, memory editors, automated bots, macros, or hacks to alter score inputs or solve puzzle boards automatically.
  • Modifying, manipulating, or reverse-engineering app code or server data packets transmitted via Microsoft PlayFab.
  • Exploiting software bugs, glitches, or data duplication vulnerabilities to artificially inflate your "Trove tile count," milestone multipliers, or daily streak loops.
  • Engaging in "time-traveling" exploits (intentionally altering your mobile device’s system clock or time-zone arrays to artificially bypass the rolling 30-day server clock or speed up daily matrix loops).

If Alchemical Solutions LLC or its automated monitoring systems determine that a player has violated these Community and Leaderboard Rules, we reserve the right, at our sole and absolute discretion, to enforce any of the following penalties without liability:

  • Leaderboard Scrubbing: Immediate removal of your high scores and permanent exclusion of your Zen Profile from all public leaderboard displays.
  • Streak Forfeiture: Instant reset and wiping of active daily streaks, milestone multipliers, and accrued fractions or whole numbers of "magic 9 tiles."
  • Profile Suspension: Temporary or permanent termination of your Zen Profile cloud-sync data, removing your ability to sync progress across multiple devices.
  • Device Banning: Restricting your unique hardware tracking identifiers from establishing a secure connection to our Microsoft PlayFab backend servers.

6. Filing a DMCA Infringement Notification

To be legally effective under 17 U.S.C. § 512(c)(3), your written notification must include substantially all of the following information:

  1. A physical or electronic signature of a person authorized to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed.
  2. Clear identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed.
  3. Specific identification of the material that is claimed to be infringing and that is to be removed or access to which is to be disabled. For 9 to Zen™, you must provide the specific Zen Profile name, a precise leaderboard placement screenshot, timestamp, and context to help us locate the asset.
  4. Information reasonably sufficient to permit us to contact you, such as an address, telephone number, and an electronic mail address.
  5. A statement that you have a good faith belief that use of the material in the manner complained of is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  6. A statement that the information in the notification is accurate, and under penalty of perjury, that you are authorized to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed.
